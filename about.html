About RW-Rulindo Agro Market

Connecting Farmers and Buyers Easily

Who We Are

RW-Rulindo Agro Market is an online agriculture platform created to help farmers sell their products easily and connect with customers across Rwanda.

Our system supports digital farming business by allowing farmers to upload products, communicate with buyers, and grow their market online.

Our Mission

To improve agriculture business using modern technology.

Our Vision

To become the best online agro market platform in Rwanda.
